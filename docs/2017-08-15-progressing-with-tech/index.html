Progressing with Tech

I've been invited as a Table Host where I'll answer your questions about the Tech industry one-to-one, this Saturday, 19 August 2017, 10am-1.30pm.

Suitable for students, fresh graduates, working adults, mid-career professionals, stay-at-home mothers, anyone and everyone keen to learn more about tech! PPIS is working with The Codette Project to highlight the ease of access into the world of technology for your personal and professional use.

Apart from myself as a Table Host, there will also be a Panel Discussion with Member of Parliament, Mr Saktiandi Supaat and some of my friends from the Tech Industry, Liyana Sulaiman (Gimmie.io), Jeff Salleh, Senior Software Development Manager at Oracle Corporation, Nurul Jihadah (The Codette Project) and Nur Iman Izam (Markedshot).

Sign up for this free event at: bit.ly/PPISxTCP.

Breakfast will be provided! Spaces are limited, so hurry!
